# Customer-Data-Analysis
Customer Shopping Data Analysis

## Project Overview
This project analyzes customer shopping behavior using transactional data collected from different shopping malls. The analysis focuses on understanding customer demographics, purchasing patterns, product category preferences, and payment methods.

The goal of this project is to generate meaningful insights that help businesses make data-driven decisions and improve customer experience.

## Tools Used
- MySQL Workbench
- Power BI


## Dataset Description
The dataset contains customer shopping transaction details including:

- Invoice Number
- Customer ID
- Gender
- Age
- Product Category
- Quantity
- Price
- Payment Method
- Invoice Date
- Shopping Mall

## Key Insights

 1. Female Customers Drive Most Transactions
Female customers contribute about 62% of total shopping transactions, while male customers account for around 38%. This indicates that female customers are the most active shoppers.

2. Female Customers Generate Higher Revenue
Revenue analysis shows that female customers generate more total revenue compared to male customers.

3. Clothing Is the Most Purchased Product Category
Clothing has the highest purchase quantity among all product categories, indicating strong customer demand.

 4. Female Customers Purchase More in Specific Categories
Female customers purchase more products in Clothing, Cosmetics, and Toys compared to male customers.

 5. Older Customers Spend More Money
Customers aged 56+ show the highest shopping expenditure, while the 18–25 age group contributes the least spending.

 6. Adult Age Group Buys the Most Products
The 31–45 age group purchases the highest number of products, making them the most active customer segment.

 7. Cash Is the Most Preferred Payment Method
Cash accounts for the highest share of transactions, followed by credit cards and debit cards.

## Business Recommendations
- Focus marketing strategies on female customers.
- Increase product variety in clothing and cosmetics categories.
- Target middle-aged and senior customers with premium products.
- Encourage digital payments through discounts and incentives.
